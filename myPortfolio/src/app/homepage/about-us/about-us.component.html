What is a portfolio?

A portfolio is a compilation of work that provides clear snapshots of your prior projects. It is more than just a list of previous projects; it reflects the portfolio owners' attitudes, abilities, and potential. It provides customers an idea of what you're made of. If a person's portfolio is appealing, it conveys the sense that he or she is dedicated and possesses a wide range of skills. As you start working on your portfolio, you may think that it is a waste of time and effort. However, you must ensure that you build a strong portfolio. It is a tool for presenting oneself to the global market.
